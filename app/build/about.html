Stay on track

By keeping a diary of what you eat, you can keep yourself on track with healthy eating habits. foodloggr makes it easy to maintain your food diary by not requiring you to enter calories for everything.

foodloggr keeps you accountable to yourself!

How does it work?

It's so simple - just enter the food you eat each day. No searching for foods in databases, no calculating calories.

You can also add your friends so you can view each other's food logs, giving you both further motivation to stay on track. Your friends can give you a or for each entry. See who can get the highest food score!
